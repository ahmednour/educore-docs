# Threat Modeling

## Purpose
Document the approach used to identify, evaluate, and prioritize potential threats throughout the EduCore architecture and development lifecycle.

## Objectives
- Identify critical assets.
- Analyze trust boundaries.
- Evaluate potential threats.
- Prioritize mitigation activities.

## Process
1. Define system scope.
2. Identify assets and data flows.
3. Identify trust boundaries.
4. Analyze potential threats.
5. Document mitigations.
6. Review periodically.

## Deliverables
- Data flow diagrams
- Threat register
- Mitigation plan
- Review records

## Success Criteria
- High-risk threats are documented.
- Mitigation actions are tracked.
- Threat models are updated with major architectural changes.
